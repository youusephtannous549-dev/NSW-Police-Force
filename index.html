Major Operation Conducted in Sydney’s CBD

August 17, 2025 · Crime & Justice · By Staff Reporter

Authorities conducted a major operation in Sydney’s CBD earlier today following reports of a disturbance inside a busy café. Police confirmed that the situation was resolved without any serious injuries.

Eyewitnesses described chaotic scenes as customers were evacuated from the area. Officers acted swiftly to ensure the safety of all civilians.

The suspect, described as a man known to police, was taken into custody at the scene. Further investigations are ongoing.
